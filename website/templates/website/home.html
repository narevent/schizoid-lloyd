The Journey

Born in October 2007 in Haarlem, Netherlands, Schizoid Lloyd emerged as a force of experimental avant-garde metal, defying conventions and pushing boundaries.

After winning the Rob Acda Award in 2008 and releasing the critically acclaimed EP Virus in 2009, the band evolved into a six-piece powerhouse. Their debut album "The Last Note In God's Magnum Opus" (2014) was released through Finnish label Blood Music and mastered at the legendary Fascination Street Studios.

The band has shared stages with Freak Kitchen, Amplifier, Bumblefoot, and Long Distance Calling, and was hailed as one of the highlights of ProgPower Europe 2011.
